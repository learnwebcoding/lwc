PHP 5.6.x Support Ends 31 Dec 2018 And PHP 7.0.x Support Ends 03 Dec 2018
https://blog.learnwebcoding.com/?p=25

In other words, PHP 5.6.x reaches end of life 31 Dec 2018 and PHP 7.0.x reaches end of life 03 Dec 2018. As a result, starting 01 Jan 2019, the supported versions of PHP will be 7.1.x and 7.2.x.
